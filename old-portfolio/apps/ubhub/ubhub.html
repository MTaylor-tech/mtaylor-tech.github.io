iOS App: UBHub

UBHub is a bespoke app for the Davis & Elkins College Upward Bound Summer Program. It allows staff to track student data, schedules, and whereabouts across multiple devices and generate reports about attendance and meal counts. It uses CoreData for data management, Simperium to enable syncing of data across devices, and the DropBox SDK for saving reports. It has been in use by the program since 2013 with annual updates.
