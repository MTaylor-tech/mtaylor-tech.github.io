The Threads of Wyrd

Threads of Wyrd: Anglo-Saxon Magick, Belief, and Practice

Step into a world where healing herbs sing of ancient oaths, runes whisper secret powers, and the boundaries between mortal and divine blur in the twilight. Threads of Wyrd: Anglo-Saxon Magick, Belief, and Practice unravels the captivating tapestry of early medieval England’s spiritual landscape—an environment where pagan and Christian threads intertwined, everyday life was steeped in unseen forces, and magick was woven into health, protection, fertility, and fate.

Drawing on manuscripts, runic inscriptions, archaeology, and oral traditions, this book reveals how Anglo-Saxon communities engaged with the mysterious and the sacred. From the poetic formulas of healing charms to the protective marks carved on doorposts, readers will discover a culture that balanced practical needs with profound spiritual insights. Detailed case studies and scholarly analysis illuminate how beliefs adapted over time, accommodating new faiths, social changes, and intellectual currents while retaining their essential power and purpose.

Whether you are a historian, a spiritual seeker, or simply curious about the human pursuit of meaning in a fragile world, Threads of Wyrd invites you to explore magick as the Anglo-Saxons knew it—earthy, evolving, and deeply human. This is a journey into the heart of a people who dared to shape their destiny through word, symbol, and sacred ritual, leaving us a legacy that continues to inspire, surprise, and resonate across the centuries.

The Tears of Tyrra

The Tears of Tyrra fantasy series

Step into the epic fantasy world of Tyrra, where ancient magic, rich cultures, and the clash of powerful forces create a tapestry of unforgettable tales. The Tears of Tyrra series invites readers on a journey through interconnected stories of heroism, identity, and destiny.

Memoirs of the Crimson Dwarf: Follow the spirited adventures of Feldspar Chilox, a young dwarf whose quest for belonging and purpose takes him from tragedy to triumph. Through heartbreak, humor, and encounters with mythical beings, this coming-of-age tale explores the resilience of the spirit and the forging of unbreakable bonds.

Memoirs of the Azure Elf: In this reflective sequel, Feldspar returns as an elf, transformed by fate and bound by grudges and debts. As he navigates the politics of war, the chaos of timeline manipulation, and his own mystical evolution, he reveals the sacrifices required to wield power and protect what matters most.

In the Snake’s Shadow: Experience the tribal conflicts and spiritual traditions of Cam’ron mac Domnaill, a reluctant chieftain caught between his ancestral roots and his duty to his adopted family. Amidst ancient rivalries and the encroachment of a centralized government, Cam’ron must reconcile loyalty, leadership, and survival.

Each story in the series weaves together personal struggles, epic battles, and intricate lore, creating a world as vast and vibrant as it is dangerous. Perfect for fans of deep world-building, complex characters, and thrilling fantasy, The Tears of Tyrra is a saga of bravery, betrayal, and the unyielding pursuit of hope.
